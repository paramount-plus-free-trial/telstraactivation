How to activate your Telstra SIM card?


If you’ve just received your new Telstra SIM card and are ready to get started, activating it is quick and easy. Whether you're a new customer or upgrading your device, Telstra provides a hassle-free online activation process through their dedicated portal: telstra.com/activate. This step-by-step guide will walk you through the exact process to activate your SIM, so you can start calling, texting, and using data in no time. Let’s look at how to activate your Telstra SIM card correctly.

Steps to activate your Telstra SIM card:

  • Go to telstra.com/activate using any web browser.
  • Select whether you're activating a Pre-Paid, Post-Paid, or replacing a lost/stolen SIM.
  • Input the SIM serial number (ICCID), which can be found on the SIM card or its packaging.
  • You’ll be asked to verify your identity using personal details (like ID or Medicare info) to comply with Australian regulations.
  • If you're a Pre-Paid customer, select a plan. If Post-Paid, it’ll link to your existing account.
  • Confirm all details are correct and submit the activation request.
  • Activation usually takes 15 minutes to 4 hours. Restart your phone once complete.
